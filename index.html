Welcome to Cherish Birth! I am passionate about mums and their partners being educated, informed, supported and loved throughout the journey of pregnancy, labour, birth & parenting. I know it can be overwhelming, but I truly believe that with the right support team you will have a birth that you can be at peace about.

My top 3 suggestions for a pregnant woman and her partner...

  1. Hire a Doula
  2. Trust your body and your instincts
  3. Trust your care provider and if you don't, find a new one!
